ABOUT

Welcome to TriTex Fashion.

Introduction

TriTex Fashion is a Buying agent of ready-made garments in Bangladesh. We are doing garments product with our current buyers knitted, woven & sweaters. Through good communication, excellent working relationships and reacting to our customers need, we have developed a unique, effective, reliable and professional operating structure and sourcing concept that ensures we retain our position in the Europe/USA market. We have high skill & experienced merchandisers, QA and Commercial team. As per our capacity & capability we take order to perfect execution.We are no compromise on quality and service.


Scope of work

TriTex Fashion sources and merchandises men’s, women’s & children’s clothing by sharing information from our valued customers. We have here committed professional & compliance factory for Knit, Woven, Sweater & accessories.

Establishment

TriTex Fashion has been established 2013. The reliable delivery dates, market conform prices and the reasonable production time lets TriTex Fashion grow fast.

Mission

We will satisfy our buyers as well as other regulatory requirements ensuring to provide quality service and products. To meet the goal we are committed to put the best endeavors and work as a team efficiently for continual improvement of the quality management system.

Vision

Our vision is to achieve business excellence by working with world class buyers for garments where quality will be the keystone of success.

FOUNDER & OWNER

Management Message

– “We are proud to introduce ourselves as a source agency working successful European and American garments buyers. We have achieved to reach the best standards in terms of quality, merchandising and production, allowing us to offer you the best service at the most reasonable price. For each customer we have individual Merchandiser+ QA for follow up development /sampling stage to shipment. So that team can increase their turn over & understand their work well. Product Selection

Compliance

We work with that factory which is able to full fill below requirement:

  • Requirement for social compliance.
    o Building approval.
    o Environment certificate.
    o Trade license.
    o Export license
    o Import license
    o Factory license
    o Fire license
    o BGMEA/BKMEA license
    o Permission for generator from local authority.
    o Latest drinking water test report.
  • Company rules, regulations and recruitment policy
    o Disciplinary policy.
    o Wage and working hour.
    o Health and safety.
    o Workers personal file.
    o Minimum wage scale.
    o Worker service book.
    o Time card/punch card.
    o Pay roll sheet.
    o Maternity benefit payment document.
    o Leave register book.
    o Accident/injure register.
    o Child labour.
    o Forced labour.

    • Quality Management System

    We have very strict and rigorous quality control procedures throughout the business. For all new customers, where required, we send our QA Officers to visit the facilities to learn and familiarize themselves with all customer specific QA techniques and procedures. As part of the Total Quality Management (TQM) philosophy, we are continuously searching for options in lowering our cost to give the best price with best quality to our customer, in order to meet the global fast fashion trend. We know commitment is the driving force for any success. Therefore we are committed to our buyer to ensure timely supply of quality products at competitive price meeting global standard in the age of global market economy.
